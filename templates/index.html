Genetic Algorithm (GA)

Genetic Algorithms are inspired by natural selection and genetic mechanisms. They use:

  • Population-based search
  • Selection of the fittest individuals
  • Crossover and mutation operations
  • Evolution over generations

GAs are effective for complex optimization problems with large search spaces.

Configure GA
Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)

Particle Swarm Optimization is inspired by social behavior of bird flocking. It uses:

  • Swarm of particles exploring the search space
  • Personal and global best positions
  • Velocity and position updates
  • Social and cognitive components

PSO is known for fast convergence and simplicity of implementation.
